Bridge Inspection Job Safety Briefing

This form is designed to document routine bridge, culvert, and retaining wall inspections conducted by the INDOT Bridge Inspection department in accordance with the INDOT Safety Manual. It applies to inspections performed on foot from the highway shoulder and underneath the bridge, where no access vehicles, traffic control, or special equipment are required. The inspections are carried out using standard personal protective equipment (PPE) in weather conditions that ensure adequate visibility.


The intent of this form is to streamline the safety documentation process for inspections conducted in favorable weather conditions that do not require the use of special equipment or traffic management. Inspections that require access equipment or traffic control will require a separate Job Hazard Analysis (JHA) to be documented prior to the inspection and attached to the associated inspection reports.
