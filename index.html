Job Safety Briefing

This form is required for all routine inspection work. Routine inspections are conducted on foot from the shoulder of the highway and underneath the bridge. Work vehicles should have their strobe lights activated and be positioned to alert approaching drivers of the inspector's presence. The required personal protective equipment (PPE) for routine inspections includes Class 3 safety apparel, work boots, and an INDOT-issued hardhat. Routine inspections should only be performed under weather conditions that allow for adequate visibility. Heavy rain and dense fog should be avoided to ensure safety. For any inspection work requiring access equipment and/or traffic control, a separate Job Hazard Analysis must be completed.
